DA-DUCK
2024
Commercial Branding Design
P5.js character goods inspired by Yeongdeungpo’s mallard, sold at The Hyundai Department Store

DADUCK is a digital character created with p5.js, inspired by Yeongdeungpo’s symbol, the mallard duck. Reflecting the district’s fast-paced energy, DADUCK captures the efficient, dynamic lifestyle of a city worker, with animations featuring quick, purposeful steps and lively head movements.

This project tests the commercial reach of code-based graphics by bringing DADUCK’s animations to life on products like hand warmers and scrubbers. Users can experience DADUCK’s animated movements on our website, bridging digital and physical realms.
