The Silver Bell Challenge: Hong Yeon-gil Project is a new media art project by Team ABT, where we worked with elderly residents of Hongyeon-gil, Yeonhui-dong, Seoul, to connect different generations through art. Despite the area's cultural richness, many elderly residents had never visited its galleries. Our project engaged them in creative activities, bridging this gap.

As the branding lead, I developed a modern visual identity and used creative coding to design dynamic web posters and a website that archives our six-month journey, showcasing the team's work.

The project won the 2023 Emerging Artists Growth Support Platform_Hwaon award, leading to a study trip to Stuttgart.
