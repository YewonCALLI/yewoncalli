Ganpan
2024
Zine Game
A digital art project transforming user-inputted text into Korean signboard images.

At the core of the project is the creation of a digital database by photographing and labeling individual letters on city signs. On the website developed based on this, users can enter their own sentences, which are automatically assembled and displayed as images of letters extracted from the signs.

"Signboard" not only provides the ability to store images of sentences created by users in digital form, but also transforms these images into analog publications in the form of zines. This brings the artistic process from the digital back into physical form (in the hands of people) and explores how contemporary digital media can work complementarily with analog. Through the visual reconstruction of the text, the viewer is invited to take a fresh look at the spatial landscape of Korea, and at the same time, it presents the possibilities of digital art (website) and publishing media.
