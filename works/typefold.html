A design tool for bridging digital typography with physical space through computational origami.

TypeFold is design tool that bridges the gap between digital typography and physical form. The project aims to solve a common limitation in Creative Coding: while code-generated graphics typically remain confined to 2D screens, TypeFold enables their transformation into tangible, three-dimensional objects.

Using p5.js, the system generates real-time textures that are seamlessly mapped onto 3D typographic forms. These forms can then be converted into paper-craft patterns, allowing digital creations to exist in physical space. The process involves sophisticated algorithms for face separation, UV mapping, and unfolding, making complex 3D transformations accessible to designers regardless of their technical expertise.
